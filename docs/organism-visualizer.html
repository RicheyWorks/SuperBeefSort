The Organism

one append-only log, many rebuildable caches RicheyWorks ecosystem · SmokeHouse doctrine

Every engine is a rebuildable cache of one truth: the segment log. Append records and watch the index, the materialized view, the replica, and the Brand provenance seal all fold off the same tail. Then crash — the caches vanish and rebuild from the log alone, identically. Or tamper a past record: CRC still passes, but Brand's chained digest catches it.
